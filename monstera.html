Monstera Deliciosa

Plant Description

Native to the rainforest of Central America, the big, bold Monstera deliciosa plant is also known as the "split-leaf philodendron". This easy-to-grow climbing evergreen can be found in many designer spaces for its "wow" factor. Indoors, the plant had a moderate growth rate and can grow in height about 1 to 2 feet a year. Its naturally glossy large heart-shaped leaves have a characteristics split. You'll also spot intricate aerial roots growing out of the soil which benefit the plant by supporting the stems that hold leaves that can grow to 3 feet long.
