About Us

Coming from our love of plants, we've embarked on this journey since 2017 with the aim of bringing nature closer to our living spaces without intimidating new plant owners and introducing the joy that plants are capable of bringing us to more people. Our company, Zinc .co started as homegrown brand name that has delivered thousands of plants and gifts on countless occasions to countless customer.

With the current pandemic, it has pushed the spread of e-commerce to new customers and product categories. It has given customers with access to a wide range of products and services from the comfort and safety of their own homes, and it has allowed enterprises to continue operating despite contact limitations and other confinement measures. Hence, we have also built a website to serve those who wish to own a plant or arrange a gift for a loved one by providing a streamlined procedure from ordering to delivery, all at an affordable price. All plants are carefully home grown with all natural fertilizers and care and after careful selection, each of them are potted in our own designer pots, and hand packed in our innovative packaging to ensure our plants reach you in its best condition, ready to be displayed anywhere in your home to brighten up your space. Furthermore, we also work with multiple shopping suppliers and have a wide selection of gifts catering to different occasions with a wide range of price.

We constantly strive to meet customer demands and satisfaction and will always welcome feedback and thoughts to further improve so that our commitment to your satisfaction is always guaranteed. Ultimately, we wish to bring joy and make people happy with our selection of products from the bottom of our hearts.

Sincerely from the team of Zinc.
